About

In February 2026, my partner Sofia had a stroke. She lost half her vision and her ability to write. When I looked for apps to help her practise at home, I found almost nothing — and nothing at all in Welsh, her first language. So I built what she needed. Stroke Sight trains the eye movements that hemianopia disrupts. ReWrite rebuilds handwriting through guided tracing. Both grew out of her daily practice and the guidance of her clinical team. These are wellness support tools designed to complement professional rehabilitation — they are not medical devices. Always follow the guidance of your clinician.

Liam Anstey, Founder
